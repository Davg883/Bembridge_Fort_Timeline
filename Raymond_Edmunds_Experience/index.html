Uncovering Island History

A project aiming to uncover the hidden histories of an Isle of Wight fort and its surrounding defences has received a major funding boost. The Bembridge Fort Trust, working with the Hampshire and IW Community Foundation and Creative Island, has launched the ‘Forgotten People, Lost Technologies’ initiative.

This project is designed to bring together volunteers, schools, and community groups to explore the Fort’s rich and secretive past. We focus on the personal stories and technological innovations that shaped Bembridge Fort’s 150-year history.

“We look forward to recording and hearing your stories and make the past live again. Let us make sure these stories are never forgotten.”

— David Reeves, Chair

Get Involved

Are you an Islander whose ancestors served, worked, or lived near Bembridge Fort, or nearby military sites? We are calling on you to come forward with any photos, documents, or memories.

Free workshops will be set up to record stories, and the team will show visitors how to restore, colourise, and even animate old photos.
