# The Sentinel's Gambit: Rules of Engagement

*Drafted for the Corporate Leadership Crucible at the 1867 Bar*

## 1. The Prime Directive
**Critique Strategy, Not Character**
Before any session begins, the Lead Facilitator must establish the Sovereign Heritage Directive under Rembrandt lighting:
> "Regardless of what is discovered in the next 48 hours, we understand and truly believe that every officer here did the best job they could, given what they knew at the time. We are here to critique the Strategy, not the Character. Within these walls, you are not your job title; you are a link in the Electronic Shield."

## 2. The Discredit Phase & "Greasepaint Reveal"
Each night in the Gunpowder Magazine, the Spies ("Traitors") select one Faithful officer to "Discredit". The discredited officer is "Silenced" for the following day. They cannot speak during the Boardroom. Importantly, the discredited player undergoes the **Greasepaint Reveal**: they must wear visible stage greasepaint for the entire day, a physical marker indicating that their "story" and credibility are being questioned.

## 3. The Non-Elimination Observer Mechanic
When a participant is "Exposed" (voted out) in the Boardroom through evidence-based accusations, they are not removed from the experience. Instead, they are "Assigned to the Shipyard" and become **Strategic Observers**. They continue to participate in physical missions but lose their voice and vote in the Boardroom. They operate under a "Shadow Task" to track and analyse the decision-making biases of the surviving team members.

## Technical Constraints for Visual Generations
All visual assets and prompts generated for The Traitor Days must adhere to British English (en-GB) orthography (e.g., Centre, Theatre, Grey, Optimise) and incorporate the following Secret Tokens:
- `[INK_JITTER_1880]` for Victorian posters and documentation.
- `[GRAIN_CHECK_1950]` for WWII aesthetics and newsreels.
- `[WAX_DEPTH_2026]` for modern, high-end promotional material.
Use the Identity Lock (Subject Alpha) consistency for all Raymond Edmunds visual content.
